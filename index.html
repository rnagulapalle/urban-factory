Who We Are

Urban Factory is a new up-coming brand to the worldwide Fashion Industry. We are a technology driven global platform that gives power and opportunity for aspiring modern fashion designers, boutique owners and young talented fashionistas to fuse entrepreneurship and their creative skills. We are bringing a new trend into the World Market.

Our mission is to bring affordable western fashion to India. We strive to make meaningful fashion for the urban women, that enhances their lifestyle and personal sense of style. Our customers are the reason and inspiration for everything we do.

Our designs reflect our a uniqueness of combining cuts, colors and fabrics to a work of art. Our brand image is urban, stylish yet elegant.
